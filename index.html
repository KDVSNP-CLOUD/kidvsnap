Install as an App
📱 On Android (Chrome)
Tap the ⋮ menu (top right of browser) → "Add to Home screen" or "Install app" → Add. KidVSnap will appear as a full app icon.
🍎 On iPhone (Safari)
Tap the Share icon (square with arrow) → "Add to Home Screen" → Add.
⚠ Important
Always open KidVSnap from that home screen icon (not by re-opening the file from Downloads, WhatsApp, or email each time). Opening it a different way can lose your saved data.
